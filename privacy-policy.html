AharSetu Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2, 2026

AharSetu respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the AharSetu mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information such as your name, email address, and login details.
  • Profile information such as age, gender, height, weight, diet preference, and health goals.
  • Health and wellness information such as prakriti details, daily routine inputs, health check responses, calorie-related data, and diet recommendations.
  • Uploaded content such as profile photo, if you choose to upload one.
  • App preferences such as notification settings and reminder timings.
  • Technical information required for app functionality, security, and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Generate personalized diet plans and meal recommendations.
  • Provide calorie calculation and health-related insights.
  • Store your preferences and improve your app experience.
  • Send meal reminders and app-related notifications when enabled by you.
  • Maintain app security, troubleshoot issues, and improve features.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data may be stored using secure cloud services such as Firebase Authentication, Firestore, and Firebase Storage. We take reasonable technical and organizational steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.

While we work to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data only with trusted service providers required to operate the app, such as authentication, database, storage, and notification services.

We may also disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal request, or to protect the safety, rights, and integrity of users or the app.

5. Notifications

If you enable notifications, AharSetu may send meal reminders and related wellness prompts. You can manage or disable these reminders in the app settings or device settings.

6. Account Deletion and Data Deletion

You can delete your account directly inside the app through: Profile → Delete Account

When your account is deleted, we aim to remove associated user data such as profile details, routine logs, health check entries, diet plans, saved results, and uploaded profile photo.

If you cannot access the app, you may request deletion by contacting: siddhisutar90@gmail.com

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the app’s services, maintain security, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, or support legitimate backup and recovery processes.

8. Children’s Privacy

AharSetu is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to independently consent to data processing. If we become aware that data has been collected from a child without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete it.

9. Your Choices

You may:

  • Update certain profile information inside the app.
  • Control notification preferences.
  • Request account and data deletion.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date on this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact:
