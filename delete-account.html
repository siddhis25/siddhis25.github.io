AharSetu Account Deletion

If you want to delete your AharSetu account and associated data, you can request deletion using the methods below.

Delete Account Inside the App

Open the AharSetu app and go to:
Profile → Delete Account

Alternative Deletion Request

If you cannot access the app, email us at:
siddhisutar90@gmail.com

Please send the request using your registered email address or mention the email address used in your AharSetu account.

What Data Gets Deleted

  • User profile information
  • Diet preference and health profile details
  • Daily routine logs
  • Daily health check responses
  • Diet plans and saved recommendations
  • Profile photo, if uploaded

Retention Exceptions

Some technical or legal records may be retained temporarily if required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or backup recovery processes.

Processing Time

We aim to process deletion requests within 7 working days.
