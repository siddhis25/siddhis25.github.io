AharSetu Account Deletion

Effective Date: July 2, 2026

This page explains how AharSetu users can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

1. Delete Your Account Inside the App

You can delete your account directly from the AharSetu app by following these steps:

  1. Open the AharSetu app.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.
In-app deletion path:
Profile → Delete Account

2. Alternative Deletion Request Method

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by contacting us at:

Email: siddhisutar90@gmail.com

Please send the request using your registered email address, or clearly mention the email address linked to your AharSetu account so we can verify the request.

3. What Data Gets Deleted

When your deletion request is processed, we aim to delete the following data associated with your account:

  • Account profile information
  • Name, age, gender, height, weight, and health-related profile details
  • Diet preference and wellness goals
  • Prakriti-related responses and analysis inputs
  • Daily routine logs
  • Daily health check responses
  • Diet plans and saved meal recommendations
  • Notification preferences
  • Uploaded profile image, if any

4. What May Be Retained

Some limited information may be temporarily retained where necessary for:

  • Legal compliance
  • Fraud prevention or security purposes
  • Backup and disaster recovery processes

Any such retained information will be kept only as long as reasonably required.

5. Processing Time

We aim to process valid deletion requests within 7 working days.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about account or data deletion, please contact:

Email: siddhisutar90@gmail.com
